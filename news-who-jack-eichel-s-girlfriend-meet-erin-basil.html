Erin Basil, the girlfriend of NHL player Jack Eichel, has been making headlines as NHL fans love to know more about their favorite player. Erin's healthcare journey led her to PerfectServe, a healthcare communications company in Buffalo, where she began working as an enterprise communications consultant in 2021. She has since been promoted to a business specialist position.

Erin is in a long-term relationship with Jack Eichel, a Massachusetts native and professional ice hockey player who currently plays for the Vegas Golden Knights.
