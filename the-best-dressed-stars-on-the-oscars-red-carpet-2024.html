The best dressed stars on the Oscars red carpet 2024

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1008 words  |  Tandra Barner

Discover which celebrities are turning heads at this year's Oscars...

    For decades the Academy Awards has been known as the most anticipated night on the Hollywood calendar, celebrating the best of the best both on and behind the scenes of the silver screen. 

    This year marks the event's 96th anniversary and with nominees such as Emma Stone, America Ferrera and Emily Blunt set to win big, we’re in for a night of serious celebration.

    Expect to see the world's most famed faces donning their most decadent red carpet ensembles, bold enough to match their star-studded status. 

    Stay tuned as we bring you all the glitz and glam, straight from the 96th Academy Awards red carpet and watch along on our live blog.

    Margot Robbie

    Margot swapped out her usual Barbie pink red carpet ensemble for a fitted Versace FW24 RTW gown with a hip ruching accent. She opted for vintage Fred Leighton jewellery in the form of a diamond and gold twist cuff and a chunky ring.

    Zendaya

    The queen of the red carpet donned a fitted Armani Privé gown and paired it with decedent Bulgari jewellery.

    Lily Gladstone

    Lily Gladstone stunned in a custom midnight blue Gucci gown, made in collaboration with Mohawk, Cree & Comanche Nations artist Joe Big Mountain. She styled her piece alongside jewels by Bulgari.

    Melissa McCarthy

     Melissa McCarthy chose a vibrant two-toned red and fushia pink gown for this evening's events. She matched her red mirror clutch to her gown perfectly.

    Greta Lee

    Greta Lee opted for a black and white look from Loewe's FW24 collection for the occasion, styled alongside jewels by Tiffany & Co.

    Charlize Theron

    Charlize Theron accessorised her silver slip dress with a stack of dazzling necklaces, multiple bracelets and drop-down earrings, all by Boucheron.

    Jennifer Lawrence

    Jen Law chose a black and white polka dot gown for tonight's event. The star paired her strapless custom Christian Dior Haute Couture dress with glistening Swarovski jewellery.

    Ariana Grande

    Ari looked insanely chic in her all-pink ruched strapless gown and matching voluminous off-the-shoulder cape. She styled her statement piece alongside a pendant, earrings in platinum with morganites and diamonds, and a diamond ring, all by Tiffany & Co.

    Florence Pugh

    Florence left everyone breathless in her silver-toned Del Core SS24 ensemble and Bulgari jewellery. Flo's plunging neckline sheer crystal embellished bodice was paired with a silky silver skirt and platform heels. 

    Greta Gerwig

    Barbie director Greta Gerwig opted for a seriously chic twinkling square neckline gown and a diamond choker necklace by Boucheron.

    Emily Blunt

    Emily Blunt stepped out in a fully sequined cream gown from Schiaparelli's SS24 couture collection, she paired her look with a lavish stack of Tiffany & Co. platinum and diamond necklaces.

    Ryan Gosling

    Ken opted for a custom Gucci black suit with a silver light-catching trim for this evening's event.

    Jodie Foster

    Jodie Foster kept things subtle yet chic in a midnight blue satin gown with ombré diamante detailing. 

    Lupita Nyong'o

    Lupita Nyong'o made a statement in a custom Armani Privé feather-fringed diamante adorned gown with jewels by De Beers.

    Emma Stone

    The actress dazzled in a custom Louis Vuitton white brocaded peplum corseted tiered gown. She styled the piece alongside a white gold, sapphire and diamond necklace and a ring from the luxury label's High Jewellery collection.

    Anya Taylor-Joy

    Anya Taylor-Joy turned heads in the most alluring Christian Dior Haute Couture mermaid-core strapless gown, complete with beaded scales. She styled her Art Deco-esque piece with jewels by Tiffany & Co. The actress wore a platinum and yellow gold diamond necklace with a Jean Schlumberger by Tiffany ring.

    America Ferrera

    America Ferrera proved that pink will forever be in style, donning a custom Atelier Versace gown in a glamorous metallic pink hue and jewels by Pomellato.

    Billie Eilish

    In true Billie Eilish fashion the songstress pulled up to the red carpet in a non-traditional ensemble but seamlessly pulled off the full Chanel look.

    Kirsten Dunst

    Kirsten Dunst opted for a simple yet seriously chic square necklined white dress, tying the whole look together with a pop of vibrant red lipstick. She elevated her ensemble with delicate jewels by Fred Leighton.

    Hailee Steinfeld

    Hailee Steinfeld channelled her inner fairy in the most gorgeous pastel turquoise chiffon gown by Erdem, complete with intricate gold flower detailing on the bodice and around her wrist and jewels by Chopard.

    Gabrielle Union-Wade

    Gabrielle Union-Wade chose the most jaw-dropping fully crystal embossed two-tiered gown from Carolina Herrera and paired it with Tiffany & Co. jewellery for the 96th Annual Academy Awards.

    Sandra Hüller

    German actress Sandra Hüller donned a custom Schiaparelli fitted black gown with artistic off-the-shoulder sleeve accents. The glamourous ensemble was tied together with a sparkling diamond keyhole-shaped broach and a Cartier choker necklace.

    Eva Longoria

    Eva Longoria exuded quiet luxury in an accentuated off-the-shoulder Tamara Ralph couture gown, a glistening Bucherer diamond necklace and a pair of matching earrings.

    Jamie Lee Curtis

    Jamie Lee Curtis kept things simple and elegant in a fitted long-sleeve black dress by Dolce & Gabbana and accessorised with a bold gold and silver cuff bracelet.

    Liza Koshy

    Liza Koshy turned heads in a deep red gilded gown complete with rose-shaped arm detailing.

    Julianne Hough

    Julianne Hough decided on a voluminous white gown with eye-catching silver and gold bust detailing from Alexandre Vauthier.

    Brittany Snow

    Brittany Snow made a statement in a vibrant yellow custom Mônot gown, a twinkling diamond choker necklace from Pomellato and Christian Louboutin shoes.

    Vanessa Hudgens

    Vanessa Hudgens made a statement in a tight-fitting all-black Vera Wang Couture gown and decadent diamond jewellery from Chopard.

    Amelia Dimoldenberg

    Amelia Dimoldenberg, also known as Chicken Shop Girl stunned in a blush pink V-neck Gucci gown, Messika jewellery and a swooping side part hairstyle.

    Laverne Cox

    Laverne Cox chose to don a gold bodice gown with a plunging neckline and a figure-hugging black voluminous skirt from Mugler's FW86 RTW collection.

    Zuri Hall

    Zuri Hall stunned in an all-gold Rony Abou Hamdan Couture gown adorned with intricate rose detailing, Le Vian jewellery and a sleek bob hairstyle.
