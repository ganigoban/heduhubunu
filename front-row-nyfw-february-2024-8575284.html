Celebrities in the Front Row at New York Fashion Week

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  722 words  |  Patria Henriques

New York Fashion Week serves as the kickoff to the spectacle that is fashion month. While the upcoming week of runway shows gives us an awe-inspiring glimpse into the Fall/Winter trends, it's often the fascinating flock of celebs, editors, influencers, and everyone in between who fill the front rows that serve as a snapshot of the must-have items for now as well as a showcase of the people who are rocking those trends. Check back here for daily updates as we round up the must-see fashions from the front row of NYFW.

Alicia Silverstone at Christian Siriano

The longtime friend of Siriano wore a red-hot dress that was totally Clueless-coded. Silverstone looked effortlessly glam in a strapless crimson knee-length dress with a corset-like bodice, a sculptural swooping neckline, and a ruched skirt. Blingy jewelry and shoes added sparkle to the ensemble.

Aquaria at Christian Siriano

It shouldn't come as a surprise that Aquaria, the winner of Rupaul's Drag Race season 10, knows how to rock a gown. Here the queen wore a strapless corseted gown that featured sheer lace paneling and a voluminous skirt. Black patent leather gloves add just the right amount of edge.

Busy Phillips at Christian Siriano

The "Busy Tonight" host and actress wore a less extreme version of Aquairia's gown, which featured a sheer black floral lace top with an asymmetrical hem and matching pants. Phillips kept her beauty classically paired back with a red lip and wavy hair.

Melanie Lynskey at Christian Siriano

Yellowjackets star and InStyle cover girl Melanie Lynskey looked like the picture of sophistication in a sleek and chic satin Siriano number that put a sultry twist on the little black dress.

J. Smith-Cameron at Christian Siriano

Succession star J. Smith-Cameron shed her on-screen style and looked regal in a tea-length black dress that had swirls of gold floral jacquard detailing. She accessorized with a simple clutch and Mary-Jane heels.

Ashley Graham at Welcome To The Amazing Mostro Show Presented By Puma

Model Ashley Graham attended the Puma show in a long-sleeved abstract tie-dye dress and Puma Mostro sneakers. The figure-hugging dress was accentuated with a puckered texture that gave extra dimension and visual impact.

Emily Ratajkowski at the Welcome To The Amazing Mostro Show Presented By Puma

Ratajkowski braved the cold wearing a long-sleeved cleavage-baring top and short shorts that highlighted her fiery red Puma Mostro sneakers. Her ensemble was equal parts sexy and sporty.

Nicky Hilton Rothschild at Prabal Gurung

Socialite Nicky Hilton Rothschild matched the venue's lighting in a ruby pink ombré dress that featured a plunging neckline for extra drama. She offset the look with crystal-embellished heels and a silver purse with a floral motif.

Joy Sunday at Prabal Gurung

Joy Sunday, star of Netflix's 'Wednesday' and Lancôme's new global ambassador, looked like she stepped out off a film set in a jewel-toned ensemble that was equal parts Mob Wife and 1970s disco.

Leigh Lezark at Prabal Gurung

Leigh Lezark, one half of DJ duo The Misshapes, is well known in the NYC fashion scene, so it was no surprise when she sat front row in a sleeveless textured green dress with black trim—complimented with open-toe black patent shoes.

Geena Rocero at Prabal Gurung

Founder of Gender Proud, Geena Rocero, wore a long-sleeved black dress splattered with violet-hued flower prints. Slicked-back hair and matching eye shadow added to the bold look.

Auliʻi Cravalho at Prabal Gurung

Mean Girls actor Auliʻi Cravalho made a statement in a tailored blood-red suit sans shirt. She accessorized her look with stacks of earrings and a silver collar necklace.

Julia Fox at Willy Chavarria

The fashionista served another one of her signature head-turning looks at the Willy Chavarria show in a barely there bodysuit with an oversized bow worn underneath a white opera coat with billowing sleeves and dramatic train. Fox topped the look off with a dramatic white silk hat, pillow purse, and pointy shoes.

Sam Smith at Willy Chavarria

Grammy Award-winning singer Sam Smith showed up at the Willy Chavarria front row in a full look from the brand. Smith looked effortlessly cool in a plaid shirt with large lapels and relaxed yet dramatic black trousers.
