Natalie Portman Pairs a Coral Skirt Suit with a Crochet Bra in Rome

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  315 words  |  Billy Koelling

Natalie Portman has been treating the streets of Rome like a runway show this week, and we can't look away.

The actress has been showing off vibrant couture look after vibrant couture look while promoting her upcoming film, Thor: Love and Thunder.

Yesterday, she attended a promo event for the movie in a sweet petal-pink ensemble that consisted of a low-rise miniskirt and matching blazer in the pastel shade by Miu Miu, and a cream crochet bra by Polish designer Magda Butrym. The bralette was handwoven on the loom by Polish artisans implementing a traditional craftsmanship technique and featured large diamante buttons at the back.

"Today in Rome, wearing @miumiu and @magdabutrym. Who's ready for July 8th?? @thorofficial," Portman captioned her post.

The Black Swan star debuted an equally colorful look Tuesday, when she attended the U.K. premiere of the Thor sequel at London's Odeon Luxe theater. Portman walked the blue carpet in a strapless red silk minidress from Dior's cruise 2023 collection. She paired the look with a bright red floral headpiece that resembled the famous style of late Mexican painter Frida Kahlo and matching sandals.
