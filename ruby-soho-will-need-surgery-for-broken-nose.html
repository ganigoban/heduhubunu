Ruby Soho Will Need Surgery For Broken Nose

 Posted on September 4, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  197 words  |  Kelle Repass

Ruby Soho was part of a previous batch of releases from WWE last year. Many fans believed the release was the best thing for her career. She will also have to undergo surgery.

Ruby Soho made her debut at AEW All Out, much to the delight of fans all around the world. However, she has not been able to do all that much, despite competing for the AEW Women’s World Championship.

Soho suffered a broken nose during her match at AEW All Out. Soho hasn’t wrestled since this match, but was scheduled to compete on October 1st during the 1PW A New Twist Of Fate UK independent show.

The promotion has now shared a video from Soho, who confirmed that she will be undergoing surgery for her broken nose. Soho noted that she’s hopeful that she will make her 1PW debut at the promotion’s February 18th, 2023 show.

We will have to wait and see when Ruby Soho will return to in-ring action. With Saraya having made her debut this week on AEW Grand Slam, the landscape has certainly changed.
