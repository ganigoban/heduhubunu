What Time Does White Castle Close & Open?

White Castle hours of operation can vary depending on the location. However, the majority of their outlets are open seven days a week, from early morning until late evening.

Is White Castle Open on Saturday and Sunday?

Yes, White Castle is open on Saturdays and Sundays, although some locations may have slightly different hours of operation on weekends compared to weekdays.
