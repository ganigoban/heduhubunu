26 best tech gift ideas for women: Cool gifts to give

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  8 minutes  |  1592 words  |  Billy Koelling

Cool tech gifts all women will love...

    Tech gifts make the BEST gifts. Whether she gets excited over a new gadget, or she's APPLE obsessed, or just browsing for gift ideas for yourself, we've searched for the top requested tech gifts for 2023. 

    From Samsung's impressive flip phone, to the Apple Watch Series 8, the new Kindle for book loving ladies, Sky Glass and Google Chromecast for the gal who's a TV-a-holic or the latest camera gadgets for budding photographers. And wait until you see the new colourway from Apple - she's going to love it.

    The best tech gifts for women

    Apple iPhone 14 - in the new yellow shade

    Say hello to the new Apple iPhone colourway - yellow. The beautiful shade joins the lineup, and features incredible battery life, a powerful dual-camera system for pro-level photos and videos, and groundbreaking safety capabilities, including Emergency SOS via satellite and Crash Detection

    £849 AT SELFRIDGES

    Sky Glass -  Ultimate TV

    Sky Glass has probably been all over your Instagram feed with celebrities and influencers showing off their new screen candy. With a pixel perfect 4K screen, you can sit close up to Sky Glass, and get a crystal-clear view of all the TV shows you love.

    What's more, Sky Glass quickly learns exactly which shows, channels and apps you love. Just say "What should I watch?" to get recommendations tailored for you. How clever is that?

    FROM £14 AT SKY

    Kindle Paperwhite 

    Released late 2021, she'll be excited for the new Kindle Paperwhite. With 8 GB, and a 6.8" display, this will take her reading experience to the next level. What's more, there's an adjustable warm light, up to 10 weeks of battery life and 20% faster page turns compared to the previous generation. 

    £129 AT AMAZON

    Apple Watch Series 8

    If you know someone who struggles with teeny tiny screens, she'll love the full screen on Apple Watch Series 8. It has the largest, most advanced display yet while still being the most durable Apple Watch ever. With Always-On Retina display, and features to measure your ECG and blood oxygen levels, the future of health has never been more convenient. What's more, Apple Watch Series 8 has a new temperature sensor that works while you sleep. Cycle Tracking can use that data to estimate ovulation dates - combined with heart rate and cycle data, it gives you a detailed view of your menstrual cycle. 

    £399 AT CURRYS

    Samsung Galaxy Z Flip4

    Wondering what to get the trendsetter gal in your life? The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip4 has been created for those not afraid to stand out. The modern revival of the flip-phone comes in new trendy colours, and the enlarged cover screen makes checking notifications or taking calls on the go easy.

    £1099 AT SAMSUNG

    Google Nest Mini 

    Improved sound quality and bass to help blast Ed Sheeran while she gets ready for work. Powered by Google Assistant, Nest Mini is on hand to help set helpful reminders and answer all your questions, from shop closing times or helping with a tricky recipe! With a custom 100% recycled fabric cover, this gift ticks the boxes as being functional and sustainable. Comes in four beautiful colours; Chalk, Charcoal, Coral and Sky. 

    £49 AT ARGOS

    Aura Digital Photo Frame 

    Aura’s photo frames make the perfect gift, allowing families and friends to share their photos and videos at the click of a button. The Smith is the newest addition to the collection, it began from a hand drawn pattern, meticulously brought to life with finely etched stainless steel. New features include built-in speakers, allowing for short video clips to be displayed on Aura frames for the first time. Whether it’s your child’s first steps, or your best friend blowing out their birthday cake candles, cherished memories can now come to life in photo or video form. Choose from The Mason Luxe, The Mason and The Smith (pictured above).

    FROM £189 AT AMAZON

    OPPO Find N2 Flip 

    The OPPO Find N2 Flip is OPPO’s latest foldable smartphone (in a stunning lilac – the colour of the season!), that brings the nostalgia and fun of the 00s into present day.

    The larger cover screen expands your view with innovative features, whilst the foldable design reimagines photography possibilities, all in a gorgeous and flawless design that you can pop in your pocket.

    Apple AirPods Max

    A perfect balance of exhilarating high-fidelity audio and the effortless magic of AirPods. With a stunning design, H1 chip and industry-leading Active Noise Cancellation, you’ll hear every note with a new sense of clarity.

    Sonos Roam

    Enjoy the unmatched Sonos experience on Wifi, and stream directly from smartphone, tablet, or computer using Bluetooth. Roam automatically connects to your home network and pairs with your phone when you're away. So all she will have to think about is what to play.

    £179 AT SONOS

    Google Pixel 7 Pro

    The Pixel 7 Pro is Google's best-of-everything phone. Powered by Google Tensor G2, it's fast and secure, with an immersive display screen of 6.7-inch and a 72-hour battery life. The best Pixel Camera yet includes a telephoto lens and pro-level features like Macro Focus. 

    £849 AT ARGOS

    Apple Airpods Pro

    AirPods Pro deliver an unparalleled wireless audio experience. AirPods Pro (2nd generation) have been re-engineered to deliver up to 2x more Active Noise Cancellation. Adaptive Transparency reduces external noise, while Personalised Spatial Audio immerses you in sound. A single charge delivers up to 6 hours of battery life. And Touch control lets you easily adjust volume with a swipe. The revamped MagSafe Charging Case is a marvel on its own with Precision Finding, built-in speaker and lanyard loop. AirPods Pro are sweat and water-resistant, making them perfect for women with active lifestyles.

    £249 AT JOHN LEWIS

    Google Chromecast

    From big blockbusters to true crime documentaries, Chromecast with Google TV is the perfect gift for the woman who is looking to lock in some serious TV time. Google TV organises everything from your favourite streaming services into a single place and the new voice controlled remote can help you search by mood or genre, so you can find your favourite rom-com easily! 

    The 'For You' tab gives the whole family their own personalised watch suggestions and Google TV’s Watchlist lets you bookmark movies and shows to watch later. You can even experience multi-room audio with Chromecast when grouping it with other Nest speakers

    Apple iPad

    Help unleash her creativity and productivity with the all-new iPad, which is colourfully reimagined to be more capable, more intuitive and even more fun. With a new all‑screen design, 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display and four gorgeous colours, it has a bigger and better display to follow your favourite Apple Fitness+ workouts using the Fitness app.

    £369 AT CURRYS

    It’s the perfect gift for the one who has a knack for losing things. Misplacing something like your purse doesn’t have to be a big deal when it has AirTag attached. AirTag — a super-easy way to keep track of your stuff.

    FROM £30 AT AMAZON

    The Frame TV brings both entertainment and art into the home. The QLED 4K and Dual LED technology allows you to experience the brilliance of lifelike colour and unbelievable detail. When users aren’t watching TV on The Frame it turns into a blank canvas to showcase fabulous art pieces or even cherished family photographs.

    Apple iMac24
