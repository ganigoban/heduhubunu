Tracey Boakye Gifts Comedian, Kyekyeku $ 2000 (Video)

 Posted on August 23, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  154 words  |  Billy Koelling

Actress cum entrepreneur, Tracey Boakye changed the fortunes of upcoming comedian, Kyekyeku by gifting him a whopping $ 2000.

According to Tracey, Kyekyeku is one of her favourite in the movie industry and has been wanting to do something for him for a long while.

Today, 8th March 2021 is the day she called him over to her new residence at Ashaley Botwe where he surprised the young man with $ 2,000 ie GHC 11,470.00. Kyekyeku could hardly find the right words to make a sentence as he was blown away by the gift.

He quickly bent the knee to show appreciation to Tracey but she declined and asked him to get seated. He could be seen in the video shedding tears of joy.
