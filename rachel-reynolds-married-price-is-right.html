Who is Rachel Reynolds from The Price Is Right?

Rachel Reynolds is a model who was born on November 4, 1982, in Mandeville, Louisiana.

She began her career in the late 1990s and has since been seen modeling Venus swimwear and was also featured six times on the cover of Fitness RX magazine, according to her CBS biography.

Additionally, Reynolds has also dabbled in acting and has had roles in How I Met Your Mother, Jack and Jill, and The Bold and the Beautiful.

Reynolds joined The Price Is Right family in 2003 and is often seen showing off the prizes contestants can win.

Is Rachel Reynolds married?

When Reynolds isn't busy on set, she can often be found at home, spending time with her family.

Since 2010, she has been married to former MLB outfielder David Dellucci.

Dellucci joined the league in 1995 after he was drafted by the Baltimore Orioles and later had stints with seven different teams, including the Arizona Diamondbacks, New York Yankees, Texas Rangers, and Philadelphia Phillies, before retiring in 2009.

Nowadays, he can be found serving as a baseball analyst for Ole Miss and SEC Network.

Reynolds often posts pictures of her husband and their daughter on Instagram to over 47,000 followers.

What is Rachel Reynolds' net worth?

Due to Reynolds' successful career in the entertainment industry, she has been able to amass a fortune over the years.

As of June 2023, she has an estimated net worth of $4million, with an annual salary of $600,000, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This estimate comes from her game show contracts as well as other business ventures.
