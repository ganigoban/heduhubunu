Kate Middleton Wore a Bright Red Zara Blazer for Hold Still Video

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  340 words  |  Billy Koelling

On Saturday night, the Duchess of Cambridge delivered a special video to mark the end of the Hold Still exhibition. Kate Middleton thanked everyone who had taken part in the photography project, and all of those who supported it. "I just wanted to say a huge thank you to everyone who submitted an image to Hold Still," she said in the video. "I launched the project with the National Portrait Gallery back in May because I wanted to find a way to allow everyone to share their stories and experiences of lockdown."

As per usual, Kate looked flawless for her appearance. According to Instagram account @katemidleton1, the Duchess of Cambridge's bright red blazer is from Zara. Duchess Kate previously wore the same look at the 2012 London Olympics.

Per the Express, Kate accessorized her jacket with a gold charm necklace by British jeweler Alex Monroe. The chic necklace is adorned with a bumblebee, etched fossils, animals, and other botanicals.
