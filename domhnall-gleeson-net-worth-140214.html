About

Actor internationally recognized for his role as Bill Weasley in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 1 and Part 2. He went on to play lead roles in Ex Machina and The Revenant. He also portrayed General Hux in Star Wars: The Force Awakens.
