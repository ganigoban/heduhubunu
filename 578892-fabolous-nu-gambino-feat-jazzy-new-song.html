Nu Gambino

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  256 words  |  Tandra Barner

Fabolous announced earlier in the week that the sequel to his Young OG Project wouldn’t be arriving on Christmas as originally planned, but instead would be dropping in February. So to make up for the unfortunate setback, the Brooklyn vet decided to unleash a new song to hold us over today called “Nu Gambino” featuring Jazzy.

Sampling Raekwon’s “Wu Gambinos” instrumental, Fab hops on this Mark Henry-produced beat and delivers his usual slick punchlines & witty metaphors for nearly 5-minutes straight. No word yet if this will see life on the upcoming tape, or if it’s a leftover from Summetime Shootout, but either way, it’s new bars from Fabo so we’re definitely not complaining.

Take a listen and let us know what you think.

Quotable Lyrics:
