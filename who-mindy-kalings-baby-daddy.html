Who is Mindy Kaling's baby daddy?

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  362 words  |  Patria Henriques

MINDY Kaling is settling into life with a new baby.

The comic actress, 41, has added to her ever expanding brood.

Who is Mindy Kaling's baby daddy?

It is unknown who the father is of Mindy's newborn baby.

She is yet to reveal his identity.

It is unlikely that she will as Mindy is notoriously private when it comes to her love life.

The Mindy Project star isn't married and reveals very little about her personal life.

When did Mindy Kaling give birth?

Mindy shocked fans on October 9, 2020 when she revealed she had given birth to a baby boy called Spencer on September 3, 2020.

She had not previously revealed she was pregnant.

Mindy shared the news during an appearance on The Late Show with Stephen Colbert.

She said: “I got something I rarely get these days, which is good news, so if you wouldn't mind sharing with our audience I think they'd be thrilled to find out something extraordinary that nobody knows up until this moment."

The Office star continued: "Yes, I'm telling it for the first time now. It feels so strange.

"I gave birth to a baby boy on September 3."

Adding that the pregnancy was a secret, she continued: "This is news to a lot of people. It's true!"

How many children does Mindy Kaling have?

Mindy's newborn brings her brood up to two.

Mindy already has a two-year-old daughter, named Katherine, who she welcomed in 2017.

The comedy writer has never shared a picture of her daughter's face, and again has never revealed the identity of the child’s biological father, saying it was important to maintain some privacy.

Explaining her decision during an interview with the New York Times in 2019, Mindy said: “My feeling is that, until I speak to my daughter about that, I’m not going to talk to anyone else about it.”
