Jackie Appiah is Not My Friend- Yvonne Nelson Drops Bombshell

 Posted on August 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  245 words  |  Tandra Barner

Yvonne Nelson has stated boldly that Jackie Appiah is not her bestie but a colleague she came to meet in the industry.

Moreover, she stated during a recent interview that Jackie is not the type she hangs out or vibes with via phone as real friendship stipulates. She mentioned this while being interviewed on Kingdom FM earlier today.

The famed filmmaker and actress said regardless of her relationship with her, she finds comments Ghanaian Dancehall act Shatta Wale made about her (Jackie Appiah) where he carelessly stated that she’s into ‘toto’ business very distasteful and appalling.

“We are not friends who will call each other or hang out but Jackie is a celebrity and so far as the work is concerned, she is a very wonderful lady”, Yvonne intimated.

In other trending stories making rounds on www.Ghgossip.com , a die-hard fan of Jackie Appiah who has fallen head over heels in love with her has sent her a voice note in her DM professing his undying love for her.

Jackie was spotted in a video shared on Instagram listening to the guy’s confession. The Ghanaian actress whose fame spread beyond the shores of Africa was flabbergasted by what the guy said as he struggled with the English language to express the mad love he has for the actress.

Her colleague from Ghana and Nigeria made fun of the video wrapped in varied responses they shared in the comment section of the post.
